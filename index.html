About RyPlanner

RyPlanner is a website that helps Ryerson University students plan out their timetables!

RyPlanner is a website that helps Ryerson University students plan out their timetables! This website is currently in development! Features still being added include:
A better UI
Automatic schedule making based on courses and professors you pick
Automatic time-based schedule making
Include professor rankings based of RateMyProfessors.com
Much more small features to come!
